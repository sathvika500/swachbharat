Swachh Bharat Mission is a country-wide campaign launched by the Government of India in 2014 to achieve an Open Defecation Free India by 2019 and to improve solid and liquid waste management by 2024-25. The mission aims to promote sanitation, hygiene, and environmental sustainability, as well as to empower women and marginalized communities. The mission has constructed over 10 crore toilets and declared 6 lakh villages as ODF
